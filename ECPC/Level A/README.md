## Overview & Preparation Tips

### Required Topics

* **Core Focus:** Most problems primarily require basic implementation or mathematics.
* **Standard Template Library (STL):** Only one or two questions require a basic understanding of STL, specifically `set` and `map`.

### Key Tips

* **Problem Comprehension:** The most challenging part of this level is accurately understanding the problem statements, as they can often be confusing.
* **Constraint Analysis:** Pay close attention to the given constraints, as they will directly determine your choice of approach.
